Startup Expert

25 November 2016 on startup, Entrepreneurship

Like many people, I was not sure whether there are cut out to start our own business. After reading the Start-up Expert's first book 'Get Ready' I have a better understanding of the challenges ahead and whether I have the character and determination to succeed.

My Notes

Alistair Milne very creatively put example of trial and error and childhood learning and fearing to fail later.

Entrepreneurship is about conquering fears, ignoring people who may doubt you and being able to constantly adapt and refine your strategy and approach.

Your playing small does not serve the world.
